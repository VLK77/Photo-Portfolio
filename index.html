About

Seeing the unseen frame.

I'm Vladimír — a photographer and drone pilot based in Prague, Czechia. My work lives at the intersection of light, space, and the quiet moments between.

Whether I'm on the ground in Prokopské údolí or 120 metres above the city, I'm looking for the same thing: the frame that makes you pause.

Available for commissioned work, collaborations, and editorial projects throughout the Czech Republic and Europe.
